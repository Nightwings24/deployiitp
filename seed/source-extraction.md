## Research interests
Mathematical Modeling in Biological Systems and Nonlinear Dynamics:
- Mathematical modeling of epidemiology and ecology using differential equations (ODE/DDE/SDE)
- Computational and mathematical analysis of nonlinear dynamical behavior
- Parameter estimation and disease-outcome prediction
- Bifurcation analysis
- Optimal control strategies in infectious disease modeling
- Coding theory / ZCCS & complementary codes (collaborative work)
